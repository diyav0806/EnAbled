Did you know?

It is believed some of the best-known scientists in history, including Albert Einstein, Isaac Newton, Ludwig Wittgenstein and even the painter of "The Starry Night", Vincent Van Gogh were believed to have had Asperger syndrome, a neurodevelopmental disorder that's part of the autism spectrum disorder (ASD) family.

Almost every child/adult with ASD is found to have exceptional aptitude in one or more creative fields, in fact, it even has a term - Savant syndrome! This is characterized by extraordinary abilities, typically in areas such as mathematics, art, music, or memory. Isn't that amazing?

We want to aid in this process of every individual with ASD discovering their self-potential through our interactive games! Remember, the activities you enjoy the most or find yourself consistently drawn to can often be areas where your true aptitude lies
